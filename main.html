Maldives


The Maldives is a breathtaking tropical paradise in the Indian Ocean, famous for its crystal-clear waters, vibrant coral reefs, and luxurious resorts spread across beautiful atolls. It's an ideal getaway for travelers seeking tranquility, natural beauty, and unforgettable sunsets.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Male City - Explore the lively capital with its local markets and historical sites.
  2. Banana Reef - A famous diving spot known for its colorful marine life and coral formations.
  3. Hulhumale Island - Perfect for relaxing beaches and peaceful surroundings.
  4. Vaadhoo Island - Witness the magical "Sea of Stars" phenomenon at night.
  5. Sun Island - Enjoy pristine beaches, water sports, and luxurious resorts.

Must-Try Foods

  • Mas Huni - A traditional Maldivian breakfast dish made with tuna, coconut, and onion.
  • Garudhiya - A fragrant fish soup served with rice, lime, and chili.
  • Fihunu Mas - Grilled fish marinated with spicy Maldivian spices.
  • Bis Keemiya - A delicious snack pastry filled with tuna, egg, and vegetables.
  • Bondibaiy - A traditional sweet rice pudding served during special occasions.
